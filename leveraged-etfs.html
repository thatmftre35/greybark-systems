Pair Strategies // bull + bear

rotation_signal

Matched bull / bear leveraged ETFs traded as a rotation. The strategy switches between sides based on regime, vol, and signal direction. Each row shows the bull and bear sides of one underlying. Sorted by the bull ETF's trailing 1-year return, highest to lowest.

// 02

Single Direction // bull or bear only

isolated_signal

Leveraged ETFs traded as a one-sided position — the strategy expresses a directional view in isolation, without rotating between bull and bear pairs.

// 03

Single-Stock Leveraged // concentrated

single_name_signal

Bull / bear leveraged ETF pairs over a single underlying equity. Concentrated, high-conviction setups where signal strength on the underlying name justifies leveraged sizing. Sorted by the bull ETF's trailing 1-year return, highest to lowest.
