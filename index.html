What we do

core_function
> Module 01

Source the data

Primary filings ingested at the source — leveraged ETF flows and holdings, 13F institutional positions, and Form 4 insider transactions. Parsed, normalized, and ticker-mapped within hours of release.

> Module 02

Build the signal

Each dataset becomes a directional signal: pair rotation logic for leveraged ETFs, position deltas and cluster moves on 13F, conviction scoring on insider trades. Calibrated against historical outcomes, point-in-time.

> Module 03

Translate to leverage

Signals get sized for the leveraged ETF tape — vol-aware exposure, regime detection, decay-managed hold periods. Strategy output is execution-ready, not academic.

How Chapel Street builds a trade

methodology

Our work centers on leveraged ETF flows, institutional holdings, insider trading activity, and federal contract data — turning these inputs into custom, data-driven strategies and datasets.

Our approach is built with a strong focus on risk management, minimizing volatility decay, and position sizing to help balance upside potential and disciplined exposure. Institutional clients include Fulton Financial Corp. and Wells Fargo.
